Our Sustainability Commitment

Sustainable Materials

We use only USDA certified organic Moso bamboo, which grows quickly without pesticides or fertilizers. It's a grass, not a tree, and regenerates from its own roots, making it one of the most sustainable resources on the planet.

Eco Packaging

All our products come in plastic-free, compostable packaging made from recycled materials. Even our shipping materials are biodegradable and printed with soy-based inks.

Carbon Negative

We offset 200% of our carbon emissions through verified reforestation projects. For every product sold, we plant a tree in areas affected by deforestation, making your purchase carbon negative.

Community Support

We work directly with small-scale bamboo farmers in sustainable cooperatives, ensuring fair wages and supporting local communities. 5% of our profits go to ocean cleanup initiatives.

Our Materials

We carefully select every material to ensure it meets our strict sustainability standards

Moso Bamboo

Our primary material is Moso bamboo (Phyllostachys edulis), chosen for its incredible sustainability and durability. This specific bamboo species:

  • Grows up to 3 feet per day without irrigation or fertilizers
  • Reaches maturity in just 3-5 years (compared to 30-50 years for trees)
  • Naturally antimicrobial properties reduce the need for chemical treatments
  • Absorbs 5x more carbon dioxide and produces 35% more oxygen than equivalent trees
  • Harvested from FSC-certified forests that maintain bamboo's natural habitat

We never use bamboo from areas where pandas live or feed, ensuring our harvesting practices don't impact wildlife.

Other Sustainable Materials

For our bristles and other components, we use:

  • Plant-based bristles: Made from castor bean oil for our softer brushes
  • Sisal fibers: A sustainable plant fiber used in our exfoliating brushes
  • Coconut fiber: Used in some of our tougher cleaning brushes
  • Natural rubber: FSC-certified, sustainably harvested rubber for grip elements
