Frequently Asked Questions

How long do bamboo toothbrushes last?

+

Just like conventional toothbrushes, our bamboo toothbrushes should be replaced every 3-4 months, or when the bristles begin to fray. The bamboo handle can be composted after use, while the bristles should be removed and disposed of separately.

Are your products completely plastic-free?

+

Most of our products are 95-100% plastic-free. For some items, like toothbrushes, we currently use nylon bristles as there is no fully viable plastic-free alternative that meets hygiene standards. We're constantly researching new materials to make our products even more sustainable.

How do I care for my bamboo products?

+

To extend the life of your bamboo products, we recommend keeping them dry between uses. For toothbrushes and bath products, store them upright in a dry area. Occasionally oil wooden handles with food-grade mineral oil to maintain their appearance and prevent cracking.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees or import taxes that may apply in your country.
